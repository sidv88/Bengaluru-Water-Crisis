Bengaluru Water Woes

Take action or face consequences

Future wars will be fought over water, it's high time we take necessary action before things get out of control. As everyone is aware, Cape Town, the capital of South Africa is facing severe drought and is nearing it's dry day. Bengaluru, the Silicon Valley of India is no far behind. Urbanization, concretization, lake encroachments etc. are a few of the major causes of man-made disasters. Commercialising Coorg, the origin of Cauvery water which the whole of Bengaluru is reliant upon for drinking purpose, is another major cause leading towards water shortage. Water is our basic right, let's fight for it. But most importantly, let's be mindful of how we spend it. Being a true-blue Bangalorean, I'm concerned about my fellow Bangaloreans and would do my bit in conserving water as much as I can. I'm hoping to reach out to a larger audience, and let's use this as a platform to share all the mindful practices in ensuring that our beloved city never goes dry.
